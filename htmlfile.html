About Us

Founded in 2005, Blooming Petals has been bringing the beauty of nature to our community for nearly two decades. Our passionate team of florists carefully selects the freshest blooms from local growers and sustainable sources to create stunning arrangements for every occasion. We've built lasting relationships with regional flower farms that share our commitment to quality and environmental responsibility, allowing us to offer our customers the finest seasonal blooms while supporting our local agricultural community.

We believe that flowers have the power to express emotions when words fall short. Whether celebrating joyous moments or offering comfort during difficult times, our floral designs are crafted with empathy, creativity, and attention to detail. At Blooming Petals, we're committed to sustainability in every aspect of our business. We use eco-friendly packaging materials, source seasonal flowers to reduce carbon footprint from transportation, and partner with local farms to support our community. Our waste reduction practices include composting plant materials and recycling containers, while our water conservation methods ensure we use this precious resource responsibly in both our shop operations and through our support of sustainable farming practices.
